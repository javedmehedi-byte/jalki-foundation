Find Us

Our Location

Visit our office at New Lambulane, Imphal East. We're located near Vaiphei Baptist Church.

By Car

From Imphal city center, head towards Imphal East. Look for Vaiphei Baptist Church - our office is nearby.

By Public Transport

Take any bus heading to Imphal East and get down at New Lambulane. Our office is a short walk from the bus stop.

From Airport

Imphal International Airport is approximately 8 km away. Taxis and auto-rickshaws are available from the airport.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with JALKI Foundation?

You can register as a volunteer through our Get Involved page. Fill out the volunteer registration form and our team will contact you with available opportunities.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to JALKI Foundation are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive an 80G receipt for your donation.

How can my organization partner with JALKI Foundation?

We welcome partnerships with corporates, institutions, and other NGOs. Please contact us through this form or email us at jalkifoundation@gmail.com with your partnership proposal.

How can I request a program in my community?

If you'd like us to conduct a health camp, training program, or awareness session in your community, please contact us with details about your community and the type of program needed.

How is my donation used?

Your donations directly support our programs in healthcare, education, skill development, environmental conservation, and social welfare. We maintain complete transparency and provide regular updates on how funds are utilized.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Join us in our mission to empower communities and transform lives. Every contribution counts, every action matters.
