Comprehensive Development Programs

Our programs are designed to address the multifaceted challenges faced by communities, creating sustainable solutions that empower individuals and transform lives.

Healthcare Services & Health Awareness

Access to quality healthcare remains a significant challenge in many rural areas of Manipur. Our healthcare programs aim to bridge this gap by bringing medical services directly to underserved communities and raising awareness about preventive healthcare.

We organize regular health camps, provide free medical consultations, distribute essential medicines, and conduct health awareness programs on various topics including maternal health, nutrition, hygiene, and disease prevention.

  • Free medical camps in remote villages
  • Health awareness workshops and seminars
  • Distribution of essential medicines
  • Maternal and child health programs
  • Mental health awareness initiatives
  • First aid training for community members

Skill Development & Vocational Training

Unemployment and lack of marketable skills are major challenges facing youth in our region. Our skill development programs equip young people with practical skills that enhance their employability and enable them to become self-reliant.

We offer a range of vocational training courses designed to meet market demands, from traditional crafts to modern digital skills. Our programs include hands-on training, mentorship, and support for starting small businesses.

  • Computer literacy and digital skills training
  • Tailoring and garment making courses
  • Handicraft and traditional arts training
  • Beauty and wellness courses
  • Entrepreneurship development programs
  • Job placement assistance

Women, Child & Disability Development

Empowering women, protecting children, and supporting persons with disabilities are central to our mission. We believe that when women are empowered, entire communities benefit, and that every child deserves a safe, nurturing environment to grow and thrive.

Our programs focus on economic empowerment of women through self-help groups and livelihood training, child protection and education support, and creating inclusive opportunities for persons with disabilities.

  • Women's self-help group formation and support
  • Microfinance and savings programs
  • Child education sponsorship
  • Child protection awareness campaigns
  • Disability rights awareness programs
  • Assistive devices distribution
  • Gender sensitization workshops

Agriculture & Animal Husbandry Development

Agriculture remains the primary livelihood for many families in Manipur. Our agricultural programs aim to enhance productivity, promote sustainable farming practices, and improve the economic conditions of farming communities.

We provide training on modern farming techniques, organic agriculture, integrated pest management, and animal husbandry. We also support farmers with quality seeds, tools, and market linkages.

  • Organic farming training and certification
  • Distribution of quality seeds and saplings
  • Integrated pest management workshops
  • Poultry and livestock development
  • Fishery development programs
  • Market linkage and farmer producer organizations
  • Water harvesting and irrigation support

Environmental Protection Initiatives

Environmental conservation is crucial for sustainable development. Our environmental programs focus on protecting natural resources, promoting eco-friendly practices, and raising awareness about environmental issues among communities.

We organize tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, waste management programs, and environmental education initiatives. We work with schools, communities, and local authorities to create a culture of environmental responsibility.

  • Tree plantation and afforestation drives
  • Community clean-up campaigns
  • Waste management and recycling programs
  • Plastic-free village initiatives
  • Environmental education in schools
  • Water body conservation projects
  • Climate change awareness programs

Poverty Alleviation & Social Welfare

Our social welfare programs address the immediate needs of vulnerable populations while working towards long-term solutions for poverty alleviation. We believe in providing dignified support that empowers individuals to improve their circumstances.

We provide emergency relief during disasters, support for elderly and destitute individuals, educational assistance for underprivileged children, and various community development initiatives aimed at reducing poverty and inequality.

  • Emergency relief and disaster response
  • Food and nutrition support programs
  • Educational scholarships and support
  • Elderly care and support services
  • Housing assistance for the homeless
  • Legal aid and awareness programs
  • Community development initiatives
