Who We Are

JALKI Foundation is a registered non-profit organization based in Imphal, Manipur, dedicated to creating sustainable positive change in communities across the region. Founded with a vision to empower the marginalized and underserved, we work tirelessly to address critical social issues through comprehensive development programs.

Our name "JALKI" represents our commitment to being a beacon of hope and transformation for communities in need. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of grassroots development, implementing programs that address healthcare, education, skill development, women empowerment, environmental conservation, and social welfare.

We believe in the power of community-driven development and work closely with local stakeholders, government bodies, and partner organizations to maximize our impact and ensure sustainable outcomes.

Our Journey

The JALKI Story

From Humble Beginnings to Lasting Impact

JALKI Foundation was born out of a deep desire to address the pressing social challenges faced by communities in Manipur. What started as a small group of passionate individuals conducting awareness programs has grown into a full-fledged organization implementing multi-faceted development initiatives.

Over the years, we have expanded our reach from a single village to multiple districts, touching thousands of lives through our healthcare camps, skill development programs, women empowerment initiatives, and environmental conservation efforts.

Today, JALKI Foundation stands as a testament to what can be achieved when compassion meets action, and when communities come together for collective progress.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through sustainable development programs in healthcare, education, skill development, women and child welfare, agriculture, environmental conservation, and social welfare, creating lasting positive change and improving quality of life.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal access to healthcare, education, and opportunities for growth; where communities thrive in harmony with nature; and where no one is left behind in the journey towards progress.

Our Goal

To reach 100,000 beneficiaries across Manipur by 2030, establishing sustainable programs that continue to create impact long after our direct intervention, and building a network of empowered community leaders who drive local development.
